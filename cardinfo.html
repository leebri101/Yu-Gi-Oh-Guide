Reading Cards


(1) Card Name

The name of the card.


(7) Card Description

The card effects are written here, which describe the monster's special effects. The effects of monsters can't be used when they are set face-down on the field. However normal yellow monster card don't have effects, and has a description but doesn't affect the game in anyway.


(4) Type

All Monsters are split into a variety of archtypes. There are some monsters with specific abilities that will have additional information, next to their type.


(3) Attribute

Every monster has its own unique elemantal attributes. Which is split into 6 different attributes; DARK, LIGHT, EARTH, WATER, FIRE, WIND. all attributes are important fro card effects.


(2) Level

A Level indicator represented by the number of stars to determine a monster's level.

XYZ monsters levels are indicated by the number of stars by Rank on left of the card.


(5) Card Number

The card's unique ID number.

Useful for collecting or identifying a specific printed card.


(6) ATK(Attack Points) DEF(Defense Points)

ATK= Attack Points

DEF = Defense Points.

The Higher the Attack & Defense Points the better when battling.
